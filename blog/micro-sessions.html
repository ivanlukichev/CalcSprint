Micro-Sessions: The Fastest Way to Improve (Without Burnout)

3 min

Long practice sessions often reduce quality. Short sessions keep attention sharp.

Three focused minutes done well beat a long distracted block that slowly turns into sloppy guessing.

Use CalcSprint in short focused rounds, then come back to the same pattern until it feels easier.

  • Practice several short rounds instead of one long session.
  • Leave while attention is still sharp so tomorrow starts strong.
  • Track consistency across the week, not just one big day.
