Two-Step Problems: How to Keep the First Result in Your Head

4 min

Two-step arithmetic is mostly attention control.

Two-step arithmetic is mostly about protecting the first result long enough to finish the second operation cleanly.

Use CalcSprint in short focused rounds, then come back to the same pattern until it feels easier.

  • Pause for a beat after step one and name the result.
  • Keep the intermediate value visually or verbally active.
  • Slow down the transition between the two steps, not the whole task.
