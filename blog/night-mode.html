Night Mode: Train Without Burning Your Eyes

3 min

Night mode reduces glare and makes evening practice more comfortable.

Lower glare helps you keep attention longer in evening sessions, especially when practice happens after a full day of work or study.

Use CalcSprint in short focused rounds, then come back to the same pattern until it feels easier.

  • Switch to night mode before your eyes feel tired.
  • Combine lower glare with shorter night sessions.
  • Use comfort settings so focus stays on the math.
