Addition Shortcuts: Make 9s and 19s Easy

3 min

The fastest mental math comes from turning awkward numbers into friendly ones.

Round awkward numbers up or down, solve the easier version, and then correct by one small step.

Use CalcSprint in short focused rounds, then come back to the same pattern until it feels easier.

  • Treat 19 as 20 − 1 and 9 as 10 − 1.
  • Look for pairs that quickly build 10, 20, or 100.
  • Prefer one clean adjustment over several tiny moves.
